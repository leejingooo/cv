Mind and Body Interaction

The inspiring talks by Dr. Chris Palmer, Andrew Huberman, and Anna Lembke have sparked my interest in the interaction between mind and body, and the way our mood follows our actions.
Currently, I am interested in nutritional psychiatry, following and studying related literature.
In terms of mind and body interaction, I am conducting research using machine learning to explore the relationship between Heart Rate Variability (HRV) and Panic Disorder, based on the Neurovisceral Integration (NVI) model.

Optimizing Healthcare Resources

I am also deeply passionate about efficiently utilizing limited healthcare resources. Recognizing that 'Doctors' time' is one of the most valuable resources, I am focused on conserving their time through the development of the "진료전에" app.
